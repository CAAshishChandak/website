Biography

CA Ashish M Chandak

At the start of his career, Ashish worked as an Equity Analyst with Morgan Stanley — one of the world's top investment banks — and as Finance Head of GVIPL in Mumbai, building a deep foundation in corporate finance and capital markets.

Thereafter, he founded Ashish Chandak & Associates as a proprietorship, channelling over two decades of expertise into a multidisciplinary practice spanning forensic accounting, information systems audit, insolvency resolution, startup consultancy, and international taxation.

He holds certification in Artificial Intelligence (AICA-Level-1) from ICAI COE, Hyderabad — completed in January 2025 (Batch 150). He is a qualified Insolvency Professional from IBBI, New Delhi, and holds credentials as a Forensic Auditor, Fraud Examiner, and Information System Auditor from ICAI, New Delhi.

With 20+ years of experience, he has extensively worked across diverse verticals advising clients in India, UK, USA, UAE, Netherlands, and Singapore.
